## What we collect

- **Account identity** — when you sign in with Google or GitHub, we receive and store your **name,

- **Subscription / billing status** — whether you are on a trial, active subscription, or expired, plus
  a Stripe customer/subscription id. **Card details are handled entirely by Stripe; we never see or
  store your card number.**
- **Account state** — trial start/end dates and whether your account is suspended.
- **[Usage analytics]** — *(if/when enabled)* basic, account‑linked product events (e.g. sign‑in,
  feature usage) to improve the product. [Name your analytics provider, e.g. PostHog.]

## What we do NOT collect

- **Your LLM API keys and your prompts/conversations.** BrainEdge talks to your chosen model providers
  **directly from your device** — your keys and the content you send to models do **not** pass through
  our servers.

## How we use it

To provide and secure the service, manage your trial/subscription, prevent abuse, and (if enabled)
understand product usage. We do **not** sell your personal data. We do **not** show ads.
