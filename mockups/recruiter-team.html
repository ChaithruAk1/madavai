What do you want to get done?
What this mockup is showing: the Recruiter is outcome-first — you describe a job, it proposes a named team rendered as an org chart (not a list), with the work-shape shown visually: Relay (a chain, ① → ② → ③) or Managed (a coordinator fanning out to members in parallel). Each role is a real blueprint with capabilities. A readiness line flags what the team needs on your setup before you hire. “Hire this team” drops it straight onto the floor. The example chips load different pre-built default teams you’d ship.
